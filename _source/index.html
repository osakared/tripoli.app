Next Generation Data Logging and Machine Control

A High-Performance Open Source Core

Tripoli is designed to be a user-friendly application that you don't have to tinker with. It also is made up of components that can be used together or individually to integrate with your existing workflows or projects, whether that's other data logging software or a Jupyter notebook made for sharing with other scientists. Tripoli comes with a rock solid time-series database designed for high throughput and reliability. Our product core will always be open source and built on best practice technologies to protect your investment.

Versatile Yet Easy-To-Use

Tripoli comes with a state-of-the-art GUI that brings the user-friendliness norms musicians, artists and developers have come to expect to the world of industrial and scientific software.

  • Cross-platform support for Windows, Mac and Linux with headless mode for embedded and server operation.
  • Highly responsive GPU-accelerated GUI.
  • Custom reporting and fields to fit in with your workflow and regulatory requirements.
